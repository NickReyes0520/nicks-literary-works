Ronie “Nick” G. Reyes

Ronie “Nick” G. Reyes, known by his pen name Nick Reyes, is an author, bibliophile, and researcher based in San Nicolas, a historic suburb of Binondo, Manila in the Philippines.

A lifelong lover of books and ideas, with a soul bound to stories, inquiry, and literature, Nick has written and composed more than fifteen (15) published works spanning diverse genres, from fiction and poetry to philosophical essays and theological reflections. His works are deeply rooted in extensive research, yet guided by creativity and imagination, making him a multidisciplinary voice in today’s literary landscape.

Nick writes to inspire people, document his studies, and share meaningful stories, facts, and viewpoints. His wide-ranging interests, from folklore, mythology, psychology, and human behavior, to philosophy, faith, and even the mysteries of the unexplained, add a unique depth and curiosity to his craft. When not writing, he enjoys filmmaking, poem and song composing, journaling, sketching, or immersing himself in music together with his beloved bandmates.

For Nick, writing is both a pursuit of truth and a journey of wonder—one that invites readers to think, reflect, and see the world in new and meaningful ways.
