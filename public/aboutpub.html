KNLW | Publishing Domain

Kuya Nick’s Literary Works is an independent publishing imprint rooted in the heart of Filipino storytelling. It exists to amplify original voices, poetic visions, and culturally resonant narratives that reflect the soul of the Philippines. Every work published under KNLW is a labor of love, curated with care, crafted with cultural pride, and released with a deep reverence for the art of storytelling.

As a domain of creative freedom, Kuya Nick’s Literary Works champions authors who dare to speak truth, explore mystery, and illuminate the unseen corners of our heritage. Whether through prose, poetry, folklore, or philosophical inquiry, this imprint seeks to nurture literary works that are both timeless and transformative.

Kuya Nick’s Literary Works is not just a publisher; it is a sanctuary for stories that matter. It is where faith meets imagination, and where every page is a quiet rebellion against silence.
