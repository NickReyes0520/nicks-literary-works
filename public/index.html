📚 Featured Books

The Numbers Between 21 and 22

Discovering My Reality Through Numerology

This book is a personal and reflective journey into the space between youth and wisdom. In this heartfelt testimony, the author unpacks a transformative period of life—where maturity meets self-awareness, and acceptance becomes the path to inner peace. Guided by the symbolic language of numerology, the book explores how numbers can illuminate our realities, reveal patterns within chaos, and help us understand ourselves with grace and intention. A story of awakening, healing, and embracing what it means to grow.

Silent Languages of Love

This explores the unspoken expressions that speak louder than words. From the gentle touch of a parent, to an employee’s devotion, to nature’s quiet generosity, this book celebrates the countless ways love is conveyed through action, presence, and care. It’s a heartfelt tribute to those who struggle to say “I love you,” but show it in every silent, profound way possible—proving that real love doesn’t always speak; sometimes, it simply shows up. Whether it’s the quiet strength of a caregiver, the resilience of a partner in hard times, or the subtle kindness of a stranger’s smile, every chapter reminds us: love doesn’t always announce itself. Sometimes, its power lies in the silence it keeps and the lives it touches.

The Significance of the Right Hand

Even if You’re Left-Handed

This is a delightfully informative journey into the everyday rituals, customs, and traditions that favor the right hand—no matter your dominant side. From military salutes and oath takings to handshakes and pledges of allegiance, this book uncovers the historical, social, and sometimes symbolic reasons why the right hand reigns in specific moments. Whether you're right-handed, left-handed, or ambidextrous, discover how these seemingly small gestures reveal bigger truths about culture and identity—with a smile along the way.

War in a Quiet Battlefield

This is a poignant tribute to those who fight their hardest battles in silence. It follows the journey of individuals who bear life’s heaviest burdens not for glory, but to provide, protect, and love. With no room for breakdowns and no audience for despair, they carry the weight alone—wielding quiet strength like a sword, defending their families, partners, and purpose through storms only they truly know. This is a story of unseen heroes, and the war they silently endure to make others feel safe, secured, and protected.

The Two Faces of Justice

The Sad Reality of Our Society

This piece exposes a painful truth: that justice often bends toward those with wealth and influence, while the poor struggle to be heard. In this bold and empathetic reflection, the author confronts the imbalance in our legal systems—where money speaks louder than merit, and silence meets those with the least. Combining real-world insights with actionable guidance, this book offers both awareness and hope, empowering readers to recognize injustices and navigate the societal maze with resilience and understanding.
